Online Purchase Scam

Online shopping scams involve scammers pretending to be legitimate online sellers, either with a fake website or a fake ad on a genuine retailer site.

Learn More

Internet Love Scam

After befriending an attractive person (who is usually foreign) online, he or she tells a tale about falling into trouble or hard times.

Learn More

Impersonation Scam

Impersonation scams involve criminals pretending to be a trusted organisation such as a bank, the police, a government department or a service provider.

Learn More

Investment Scam

Investment scams involve promises of big payouts, quick money or guaranteed returns. Always be suspicious of any investment opportunities that promise a high return with little or no risk

Learn More

Credit Scam

Criminals can obtain credit cards by either finding them after they have become lost or stealing them from someone's possession. The thief may not be able to use the lost or stolen card at a point of sale device, which requires a PIN.
